About Me

Welcome to my online portfolio! I'm Lucas Plant.

I am a senior at Georgia Tech studying Electrical Engineering, with specializations in Robotics, Control Systems, and Sensing. In addition, I am pursuing a minor in Computing and Intelligence, to explore algorithms and machine learning concepts.

My research and professional experience spans aerospace, automotive, and academic labs. I have interned at SpaceX as an Avionics Test Engineering Intern for the Falcon rocket and at Tesla, as a Software Integration Intern for the Steering Team. At Georgia Tech, I contribute to the Dynamics and Control Systems Laboratory, focusing on SLAM software development, hardware integration, and robotic software.

Technical Interests

  • Optimal control and state estimation for complex, uncertain systems
  • Machine learning and AI for computer vision and robotic decision making
  • Electromagnetism for sensing and communication
  • Interactive scientific visualization, creating tools that make complex systems intuitive to explore
